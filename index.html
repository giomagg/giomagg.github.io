Hi there!

I am Giovanni Maggi, thank you for visiting my website. I am a computational researcher of the digital space with a keen interest on understanding the interaction dynamics of the online public sphere. My work is at the crossroads of political communication, sociology, and political sciences, focusing on the study of polarization, disinformation, and extremism in hybrid media systems. I investigate these topics using computational social science techniques, scraping digital trace data and modelling the digital space using a combination of network anaysis and statistical text analysis.
