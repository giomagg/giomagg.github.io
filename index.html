Hi there!

I am Giovanni Maggi, thank you for visiting my website. I am a computational researcher of the digital space with a keen interest on understanding the communication dynamics of the online public sphere. My work is at the crossroads of political communication, sociology, and political sciences, with a focus on polarization and disinformation in hybrid media systems. I study these topics using computational social science techniques and I have a particular relation to network anaysis, text analysis, and scraping methods.
